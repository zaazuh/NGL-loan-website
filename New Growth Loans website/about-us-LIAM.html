Our History

New Growth Loans emerged onto the South African financial scene in 2012 with a singular mission: to provide accessible and responsible lending solutions to all South Africans, regardless of their circumstances. Founded on principles of integrity and social responsibility, New Growth Loans quickly established itself as a beacon of hope for individuals in need of financial assistance.


From the outset, New Growth Loans differentiated itself by offering a diverse range of loan products tailored to address various needs of its customers. Whether it was financing educational pursuits, covering medical expenses, or facilitating vehicle repairs and purchases, New Growth Loans stood steadfast in its commitment to serving the diverse needs of the South African community.


Driven by a vision of a future where financial barriers were dismantled, New Growth Loans became synonymous with empowerment and opportunity. The company's unwavering dedication to responsible lending practices, coupled with its emphasis on financial education and empowerment, earned it the trust and loyalty of countless South Africans.


Over the years, New Growth Loans witnessed remarkable growth and success, expanding its reach and impact across the country. Through strategic partnerships and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction, the company solidified its position as a leader in the financial services sector.


Despite the challenges and fluctuations in the economic landscape, New Growth Loans remained steadfast in its commitment to its core values. Each interaction with customers was seen as an opportunity not only to provide financial assistance but also to educate and empower individuals to make informed financial decisions.


Today, New Growth Loans stands as a testament to the power of perseverance, integrity, and compassion. With a rich history rooted in service and social responsibility, the company continues to pave the way for a brighter, more inclusive financial future for all South Africans.

Case Studies

If Walter White chooses a loan of R20 000 and selects to repay it over 36 months, he will firstly have to pay a registration fee of R600,00 and a monthly service fee of R65. With an annual rate of 25%, his monthly instalment of the loan will be R621,00. The total repayment amount after the 36 months will be R27,936.

NOTE THAT THE ABOVE IS JUST AN EXAMPLE, INFORMATION CAN VARY BASED ON CUSTOMERS CREDIT SCORES AND MANY OTHER ASPECTS!

IMPORTANT: Our interest rate will never exceed the bar set by the NCA (National Credit Act) of 29,25%. The fees could change depending on the client/customer status.
